Development Plan

In addition to the Continuing Education Institute, part of which is the English Language Academy, and the seven currently operational colleges at the American University of Iraq – Baghdad (AUIB), several more will open in the coming few years. Of these will be the College of Agriculture, Food & Environmental Sciences, College of Medicine, College of Education, College of Engineering, College of Architecture, College of Media Studies & Communication, and the College of Performing and Fine Arts.  

Moreover, as part of its efforts at establishing itself as comparable to a Tier One Research University, AUIB hosts a growing number of research, educational, and cultural centers. In addition to offices of institutions such as UNESCO, Konrad Adenauer Stiftung, and the Iraq Energy Institute- already present on campus_ of the institutions expected to be established on campus are a National Center for Environment, Water Management, and Climate Change, a National Center for Performing and Fine Arts, and a National Center for Youth and Sports.
