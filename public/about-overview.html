About AUIB

Our History

The American University of Iraq-Baghdad (AUIB) is a nonprofit institution of higher learning that was founded in Baghdad in 2018 and opened its doors to its first class of students on February 14, 2021.

The motivation behind the establishment of AUIB began from a vision to reestablish Iraq, and particularly Baghdad, as the heart of higher learning, and as the capital of education and culture in the Middle East, especially as Baghdad was all but destroyed in the face of continuous strife and wars over the past several decades.

Founders Message

We believe no country can develop and flourish without modern education, which is the foundation upon which a nation and its communities’ institutions are built. Iraq has suffered and persevered through myriad challenges, but there is now a glimmer of hope because of the hard work of Iraqis – a new start for a promising future.

My name is Saadi Saihood, and my sons and I have made it our family’s mission to usher our country out of its legacy of terrorism and turbulence. The idea of establishing the American University of Iraq – Baghdad came to us at a family gathering with my sons, Hussein, the eldest; Ali, the middle; and Yousif, the youngest. We have worked day and night to create a contemporary university with a solid foundation of exceptional faculty and staff and partnerships with some of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the United States. This university will create a new foundation for Iraq and become the first of its kind in Baghdad, and the largest academic and research institution in the Middle East.

AUIB Leadership

Leadership at AUIB consists of the Board of Trustees, President’s Office, Vice-Presidents’ offices, Office of the Provost, and the office of the Chief Academic Officer. 
The President reports directly to the Chairman of the Board of Trustees. The Board consists of 11-15 individuals, primarily from Iraq and the United States. The Board is divided into various committees and subcommittees that work with the President to govern the institution. AUIB is proud of its diversity, with members of its administration coming from the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada, holding high-level degrees from top universities, mostly in the U.S. and Europe.  
The President of AUIB is the Chief Executive Officer of the institution. Directly reporting to him are the Vice-President of Academic Affairs, the Vice-President of Administration and Finance, the Vice-President of Institutional Advancement, the Counselor to the President, and the CEO of the Continuing Education Institute.

Why Study at AUIB

What distinguishes an American-Style university education is adherence to the liberal arts tradition, a strong focus on developing each student’s original and critical thinking skills, emphasis on STEM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), and the use of standardized testing, thus promoting creativity, technological development, and data-driven decision-making. Nonetheless it is important to tailor the American education system to meet the unique characteristics and needs of countries in Iraq and the region, which is exactly what AUIB does, as it roots itself in Baghdad’s great academic and cultural heritage.
