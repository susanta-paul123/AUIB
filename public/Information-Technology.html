Information Technology

The hallmark of AUIB’s Information Technology (IT) department is to secure specialized skills and personnel of the highest caliber to deliver and maintain the high-end IT solutions and services to the AUIB family, keeping the environment operational, productive, agile, innovative, and continually on the forefront of the educational structures. Planning is continuous to sustain AUIB’s IT systems as the finest example of state-of-the-art, cutting-edge instructional technology in Iraq. As these technologies evolve and improve, so does AUIB’s adoption of these technologies, to ensure that students enjoy the best possible instruction available, potentially from professors at universities around the world.  IT department’s highly skilled personnel provide a wide range of services to adapt and develop the latest trending IT technologies, along with IT core infrastructure managed service provided by AUIB’s strategic partner, Al Tameer IT Solution. IT guarantees fully integrated systems that are robust and with highest serviceability to meet AUIB end-user satisfaction and expectations.  As part of our strategy to maintain cutting edge technologies at AUIB, our IT department manages the following systems in support of administrative, instructional, and security-related services: 

  • Academic & Administration Solutions:

    • OracleERP & PeopleSoft SIS
    • Canvas Learning Management Solution
    • TurnitinReal-Time Anti-Plagiarism Solution

  • Library Management Solution:

    • WorldShareManagement System
    • ProQuestReal-Time Insight Management System
    • Bibliotheca RFID System
    • Microsoft 365

  • IT Infrastructure Systems

    • ManageEngin, Proffisional, Helpdesk System
    • Xerox Printing System
    • Time & Attendance System
    • Dahua Smart CCTV System
    • Crestron&CleverTouchAudio/Video System

  • Cisco Environment

    • Core Infrastructure
    • Call Manager & IPT System

  • Huawei Environment

    • Distribution & Access Infrastructure
    • Indoor WIFI Coverage
    • Outdoor WIFI Coverage

  • IT Support Services

    • Helpdesk Support Services With Sharp SLA
    • Field Support Services
    • Network Operation Center – NOC
    • Security Operation Center – SOC
    • IP Telephony Support Services
    • LAN Support Services
    • WIFI Support Services
    • Internet Support Services

    • CCTV & Audio/Video Systems Services
