ABOUT AUIB

The American University of Iraq-Baghdad (AUIB) is a non-profit, private, co-educational institution of higher learning, that is fully accredited in Iraq by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. The university is governed by an international group of administrators, headed by an independent board of trustees, which is divided into various committees and subcommittees that work with the President, Vice Presidents, Deans of Colleges, Division Directors and Departmental Chairs to provide students with excellence in education.
