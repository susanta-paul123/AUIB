Why Study at AUIB’s CIS?

The College of International Studies (CIS) at AUIB has designed its interdisciplinary curriculum and learning outcomes to facilitate the journey of CIS students along a lifelong path of academic enrichment and professional development. A talented roster of international faculty members, drawn from around the globe, engage students in courses, classrooms, and in research, in order to prepare them for the wider world of International Affairs and International Security. The future leaders, diplomats, and policymakers of Iraq

And the larger region will examine the historical, political, economic, and ideological factors that combine to influence international politics and foreign policy frameworks. Students learn theory and practice, and can specialize in fields as diverse as International Political Economy, International Organization, Public Policy, Comparative Politics, Global Area Studies, and International Law & Human Rights. The College’s state-of-the-art facilities support high-impact research, international communication and networking, and innovation.

Graduates’ Prospective Careers

CIS graduates will be highly qualified to find dynamic and rewarding jobs and opportunities in the following areas:

  • Diplomatic Corps
  • Regional and International NGOs
  • International Research and Higher Education
  • Government and Public Policy
  • Human Rights Organizations
  • Defense Departments, Military, and Intelligence Agencies
  • International Trade and International Business

Values

  • Academic Excellence and Intellectual Freedom
  • Professionalism and Personal Integrity
  • Strong Sense of Community and Pride in their Homeland
  • Inclusion and Openness
  • Diversity in Experience, Thought, and Engagement
  • Sustainability and Protection of the Planet
  • International Trade and International Business
  • Self-exploration and Discovery

Programs

CIS offers an interdisciplinary curriculum that examines international and political issues within specific geographical, cultural, historical, philosophical, and social contexts, offering Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree programs in:

  • International Relations and Security Studies (IR&SS)
  • Government and Public Policy
  • Political Science

Our primary program, IR&SS, reflects areas of global concern that impact the daily lives of everyone on the planet. The BA program in IR&SS launches students on a career that deals with subjects and people of global importance, and trains them in a profession that is dedicated to solving complex problems and challenges in Iraq, the Middle East, and in other regions of the world.

Our students prepare themselves for careers in both domestic and foreign affairs, or international business, as well as the International NGO sector.

Faculty

CIS boasts a team of highly competent and culturally diverse international faculty members who hold highest level degrees from renowned academic institutions in the United States and Europe, as well as top institutions around the world.

CIS faculty are recognized for their outstanding teaching capabilities, well-established scholarship, and international outreach and collaboration.

At the College of International Studies at the American University of Iraq-Baghdad, and together we can help change the world!
