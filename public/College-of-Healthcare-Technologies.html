Why Study at AUIB’s CHT?

The College of Healthcare Technologies (CHT) ushers in the next generation of healthcare professionals and specialists, enabling our communities to live better and healthier lives, served by experts who use the latest technologies and cutting-edge treatment approaches. Professionals from a wide variety of specialties deliver medical and rehabilitative healthcare training, supported by classroom instruction, direct clinical experience, and research, employing modern healthcare applications to better diagnose and effectively treat patients.

Graduates’ Prospective Careers

With a Bachelor’s degree from CHT, graduates will be well trained, competent, and eligible to practice locally, regionally and worldwide after securing country-specific licensing requirements. They will be highly qualified and in demand to work as skilled professionals in the following fields:

Radiology & Sonar

  1. Hospital imaging departments
  2. Private and public medical imaging departments
  3. Education and research
  4. Application specialists in medical imaging/ radiation equipment and supplies

Optometry & Vision Science

  1. Hospital imaging departments
  2. Private and public medical imaging departments
  3. Education and research
  4. Application specialists in medical imaging/ radiation equipment and supplies

Dental Technology

  1. Hospital imaging departments
  2. Private and public medical imaging departments
  3. Education and research
  4. Application specialists in medical imaging/ radiation equipment and supplies

Anesthesia Technology

  1. Hospital imaging departments
  2. Private and public medical imaging departments
  3. Education and research
  4. Application specialists in medical imaging/ radiation equipment and supplies

Values

CHT recognizes and fosters student Talent, in a spirit of Integrity, Professionalism, Teamwork and Collaboration, and strives for Excellence in education and research. The latter is driven by Creativity and Innovation, themselves fueled by Scientific Curiosity and a commitment to Lifelong Learning.  

Our Distinguished Programs

CHT offers top programs that allow rapid employment in high demand fields. Our programs are designed to give our students an edge to have a successful career in healthcare. The finest international faculty members, with extensive expertise in their fields, help prepare CHT graduates to become the leading professionals of the future.

The college currently offers four exciting120-cedit undergraduate programs:

  • Radiology & Sonar
  • Optometry & Sonar
  • Dental Technology
  • Anesthesia Technology

Future Programs

To remedy marked deficiency in quality professionals in allied and rehabilitative health sciences, CHT plans to offer soon eight new programs that will address relevant needs, and open the door for new employment opportunities. Among these programs are:

  • Health Informatics
  • Nutrition & Food Science
  • Speech Pathology
  • Audiology
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Physical Therapy
  • Clinical Laboratory Science
  • Forensic Science

International Partnerships

As part of AUIB, CHT has a number of signed partnership agreements on student and faculty exchange and research internships with leading US and Europeans universities and institutions. The College is working to finalize more partnerships with world-class universities, to offer students an unrivaled study, practice, and research experience. 

Faculty

CHT boasts a team of highly competent and culturally diverse international faculty members who hold highest level degrees from renowned academic institutions in the United States and Europe.

CHT faculty are recognized for their outstanding teaching capabilities, well-established scholarship, and international outreach and collaboration.
