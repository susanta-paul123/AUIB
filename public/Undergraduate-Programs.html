Undergraduate Programs

We currently offer more than 20 programs and continue to build the university of tomorrow for students. You can learn about our undergraduate programs and majors, along with any additional program specific admission requirements if applicable. If you require help in selecting or applying for your chosen program, we are here to help.

Bachelor of Accounting

You are interested in accounting and financial management gathering, analyzing and communicating financial information that is crucial to every aspect of business. Then you should consider Accounting. Learn from professors and experts in the field putting theory into practice.

Bachelor or Anesthesia Technology

The anesthesia program prepared student for this specialized position and role in numerous medical operations. Gain understanding of procedures and safety regulations, as well as how to assess, monitor, and the safe and effective care for patients. Classroom instruction combined with clinical education and field experience students will learn supplementary therapeutic interventions.

Bachelor of Arts - English Literature

The program provides important perspectives reflecting on worlds through time. Students examine history, philosophy, and many literary modes. The English literature program equips students to read analytically, speak comprehensibly, research, explore theories of interpretation, write with authority, and inspire.

Bachelor of Arts - Psychology

Curious about how people think, feel, and behave? Psychologists seek to understand and learn how we grow, develop, respond to the world around us, our social interactions with others, and sense of well-being. This program covers a broad range of topics from positive psychology, neuroscience, abnormal psychology, developmental psychology, aging, and culture.

Bachelor of Dental Surgery

Dentistry is a challenging and rewarding career within the healthcare field and requires a sound knowledge of related biological sciences. With state-of-the art facilities you will have an ability to give back to the community while practicing. This degree takes 5 years of full-time study to complete, not including any English language preparation.

Additional admission requirements may include: a Dental Aptitude Test (DAT), Medical Testing and Immunization, Mathematics Admissions Test (MAT), and Pre-admission Sciences Assessment (PSA).

Bachelor of Dental Technology

As a master of their craft blending technical skills and artistry, this profession prepares you for a commitment to advance oral health and community service. You will develop the specialized skills to design and fabricate various oral devise as prescribed by dental practitioner to their patients.

Bachelor of Entrepreneurship

Are you seeking to start your own business or wanting a broad based knowledge of management to focus on the opportunities and challenges of entrepreneurship. You will have access to integrative experiential learning opportunities allowing you to put theory into practice.

Bachelor of Finance and Banking

This board area of study includes investments, portfolio management, corporate finance, banking, securities, mergers and acquisitions, along with international and financial markets. The program's best professional and ethical practices ensures student have the tools necessary to thrive in the finance and banking industry.

Bachelor of International Studies

Looking for a career in diplomacy and government, students examine international and political issues, international institutions, learning and actively participating in dialogue on important ideas. The program draws on diverse disciplines including international relations, political science, global affairs, strategic studies, along with public administration and public policy.

Bachelor of Law

Our program provides student with a solid foundation in legal fundamentals in both private and public law. Acquire understanding of the dynamics of law, its agents, institutions, and power structures. Students will be able to specialize in areas through the wide array of elective courses on offer.

Bachelor of Management Information Systems

This program gives you an opportunity to apply your computer science knowledge in any business environment. Learn about the latest science and digital technologies processing, managing, and communicating information using databases, networks, telecommunications, and data visualization.

Bachelor of Management

Learn to solve today's business challenges and societal challenges, navigating through the rapid changing operating environment. This program is for those wanting a more generalist degree with a breadth of courses in finance, marketing, organizational behavior, strategic planning, and management. This degree will help you develop critical thinking, analysis, research skills, and teamwork to adapt to any career.

Bachelor of Marketing

Combining research-based principles you'll participate in market simulations, research, group projects, and case studies while developing practical skills preparing you to engage customers in the marketplace. You will be qualified to develop marketing strategies across multiple modes of communication and distribution grounded in marketing analytics.

Bachelor of Optometry and Vision Science

Become a professional in visual optics, visual neurophysiology, and clinical techniques in support of the health of vision. Take courses covering epidemiology, pharmacology, molecular biology, and apply your knowledge in clinical technology through clinical practice. Program specialties include binocular vision, contact lenses, electrodiagnostic, and ocular health.

Bachelor of Pharmacy

Learning from award winning and world-renowned faculty at the forefront of clinical innovation, you will be part of a new generation of pharmacists in the industry. We provide areas of study in pharmaceutics, pharmacokinetics, pharmacology, regulatory and policy, and access to diverse experiential placement opportunities prior to program completion. The Bachelor of Pharmacy takes 5 years of full-time study to complete, not including any English language preparation.

Additional admission requirements may include: a Dental Aptitude Test (DAT), Medical Testing and Immunization, Mathematics Admissions Test (MAT), and Pre-admission Sciences Assessment (PSA).

Bachelor of Radiology and Sonar

Work in a variety of healthcare settings applying your specialized skill in x-radiation images using highly technical equipment and imaging procedures aiding in the diagnosis of injury or disease. The program provides hands-on clinical experience in support of classroom teaching covering a broad range of imaging procedures.

Bachelor of Science - Biology

For those who have varied interests in life sciences, a Bachelor of Science majoring in biology is a good fit. This degree is designed to provide you with a broad view with fundamental concepts and a diverse range of courses in ecology, chemistry, biotechnology, and immunology.

Bachelor of Science - Chemistry

The science of chemistry is interconnected with all scientific disciplines and the materials that make up our physical world. You will have access to courses in general, organic, and environmental areas of this field of study paired with training in our state-of-the-art laboratory facilities.

Bachelor of Science - Computer Science

Use your creativity with technology to benefit society. Learn how to improve computers, databases, operating system, software, numerical methodology, and security. You will take courses in machine learning, data mining, computation, and design and analysis as part of the Computer Science program.

Bachelor of Science - Physics

Understand how matter and energy work by discovering the physical laws of nature. Our major in physics offers a wide variety of courses in astrophysics, quantum mechanics, computational physics, and electronics. This program prepares students to pursue careers in teaching, industry, research, and in diverse interdisciplinary domains. The solid professional skills, competencies, and physics knowledge acquired, prepare students to pursue graduate studies in many science and engineering disciplines.
