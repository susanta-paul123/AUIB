Academics Success Program

The Academic Success Program (ASP), a component of the ELA, is designed for students who have been conditionally admitted to AUIB for university studies. It contains six levels of study. However, students will be given a placement test first to determine the level at which they will begin. Qualifying for study at AUIB may only require one level for an advanced student, whereas a beginner may require all six levels. Students who test at the required level of proficiency can start academic studies at AUIB immediately. The ELA follows the common European framework of reference for languages (CEFR) which sets the standards for language proficiency internationally.

In addition to academic English, AUIB offers math instruction, test preparation, study skills, and other skills students will need for success at AUIB or universities abroad. Throughout the program, information technology (IT) skills are integrated into the curriculum to ensure the student is “computer literate” when the academic program begins.

What ASP offers:

  • Small class sizes of 20 students or less.
  • State-of-the-art classrooms with Smartboards, student laptops, and other technology.
  • Qualified faculty comprised of native English speakers with master’s degrees and higher.
  • Engaging and interesting textbooks with online apps to practice outside the classrooms.
  • Safe and comfortable classrooms in a beautiful and highly secure campus setting.
  • Student activities and clubs for students to join on campus.
  • Free tutoring for enrolled students.
  • Access to all on-campus facilities.

Tutoring

Often, students need additional help outside of the classroom. Instructors will be available at different times to provide additional assistance in grammar, reading, writing, or other related areas to ensure student success in the classroom.
