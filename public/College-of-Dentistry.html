College of Dentistry (CoD)

Home Academies College of Dentistry (CoD)

Why AUIB’s CoD?

CoD offers students one of the most sought-after international professional skills. The College provides contemporary educational programs that prepare dentists and dental specialists dedicated to the public good and to lifelong learning. The College’s mission is to provide Iraq and the region with quality dental professionals that meet their dental health needs in the modern world.

Graduates’ Prospective Careers

CoD graduates will be furnished with the necessary training, and will be competent and eligible to practice worldwide upon completion of the degree and additional training. They will be highly qualified to pursue diverse exciting and rewarding career paths, such as:

  • Practicing dentists
  • Research scientists
  • Government and NGO roles
  • Clinical research coordinators/associates
  • Higher education
  • Regulatory affairs and quality assurance
  • Dental care marketing and management

Values

Foremost of CoD values are Integrity, Respect, Trust, and striving for Excellence. The latter requires Teamwork, Collaboration, High-Impact Leadership in advancing dentistry education and practice, Innovation and Creativity in research and clinical services, and, last but not least, Lifelong Learning. 

Our distinguished programs

Focused on patient-centered services, the unique Dentistry curriculum combines the Core Liberal Arts component of the American educational system with in-depth practical training in the profession, to develop dentists who master interpersonal skills, added to their technical prowess, thus advancing a holistic approach to patient care. 

CoD currently offers a five-year Bachelor of Dentistry (BD) program that incorporates solid science education, coupled with robust and diverse clinical education and internships, to ensure that graduates are well-equipped for the profession’s practice in various specialties, while maintaining the highest ethical values.

Our Future Programs

In order to prepare its graduates for practice globally, in addition to Iraq and the region, CoD plans to offer a two-year post-graduate program in Dental Surgery, so that graduating Dentists will have the required education and practice equivalent to the Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) or Doctor of Medicine in Dentistry (DMD) degree, enabling them to practice in countries requiring this degree, such as the US and Canada.

The College offers several post-graduate Certificate Programs as well, to further develop existing dentists’ skills, helping them maintain competence and competitiveness in a rapidly developing technological realm. The College will also offer graduate programs leading to the Master of Science in Dentistry degree in the various dental specialties, designed for practitioners who wish to master research in the field.

International Partnerships

As part of AUIB, CoD has a number of signed partnership agreements on student and faculty exchange programs and research internship programs with leading US and European universities and institutions. The College is working to sign more partnerships with world-class universities, to offer students an unrivaled study, practice, and research experience.

Faculty

CoD boasts a team of highly competent and culturally diverse international faculty members who hold highest level degrees from renowned academic institutions in the United States and Europe.

CoD faculty are recognized for their outstanding teaching capabilities, well-established scholarship, and international outreach and collaboration.
