College of Business (CoB)

Home Academies College of Business (CoB)

Why Study at AUIB’s CoB?

The Mission of the College of Business is to ensure excellence in business education, through rigorous and applied business experience. The College fosters an integrated, experiential, and personalized learning environment, and encourages critical thinking, quantitative reasoning, effective communication, ethical decision making, social responsibility, and lifelong learning.

The College aims to establish itself as a leading regional business school, and to accelerate Iraq’s progress by developing students’ talents and graduating business leaders through the creation of knowledge promoted by data-informed decision making.

Graduates’ Prospective Careers

CoB Programs qualify their graduates to assume different roles in private businesses, community organizations, international organizations, or the public sector. Depending on the chosen program, and to give but a few examples, a graduate may work as a public or private accountant, financial controller, Chief Financial Officer, financial consultant, business, marketing, or sales officer or manager, marketing strategist, Chief Executive Officer, economic analyst or consultant, banker, financial or market analyst, portfolio manager, project manager, human resources officer or manager, and customer relations officer or manager. 

CoB also has a Management Information Systems program, which combines knowledge in business with proficiency in Information and Communications Technologies, thus qualifying graduates to work as developers (programmers), database or network administrators, security experts, or project managers, among other possible roles in the wide world of Business.

CoB Programs

The pillars of our curriculum and activities are Engagement, Innovation, and Impact. Faculty and students are in constant contact with the business community through field visits, guest lecturers, training, and mandatory internships. Faculty members are active researchers and consultants, thus transferring their updated knowledge to the classroom. 

The educational philosophy of the business programs is to provide a unique combination of academic rigor and applied learning experience. The curriculum is designed to produce forward-thinking business leaders, who think not only of profit as a primary goal, but also the general welfare of people. It prepares students to be critical thinkers, eloquent communicators, and problem solvers, using dynamic teaching approaches in an ever-changing business environment.

Bachelor’s in Accounting

Accountants are integral elements of any company. AUIB’s accounting graduates learn to assess the company’s financial well-being accurately and ethically. Their degree combines courses and topics that are internationally recognized as essential for the accounting practice and for further graduate studies in accounting.

Among other courses, students study financial accounting, cost accounting, accounting information systems, auditing, financial statement analysis, and taxation. They learn how to analyze a company’s financial statements and records in conformity with national and international accounting standards. It is a perfect field for individuals who are attentive to details and love to work with numbers.

Faculty

CoB boasts a team of highly competent and culturally diverse international faculty members who hold highest level degrees from renowned academic institutions in the United States, Europe, and abroad, and combine academic rigor and hands-on practical experience as consultants, managers, bankers, and specialists in fields such as Human Resources Management.

CoB faculty members have been awarded international recognition for their published research, and have engaged in consultancies with prominent international agencies and multinational corporations.
