Founders’ Message

We believe no country can develop and flourish without modern education, which is the foundation upon which a nation and its communities’ institutions are built. Iraq has suffered and persevered through myriad challenges, but there is now a glimmer of hope because of the hard work of Iraqis – a new start for a promising future.

My name is Saadi Saihood, and my sons and I have made it our family’s mission to usher our country out of its legacy of terrorism and turbulence. The idea of establishing the American University of Iraq – Baghdad came to us at a family gathering with my sons, Hussein, the eldest; Ali, the middle; and Yousif, the youngest. We have worked day and night to create a contemporary university with a solid foundation of exceptional faculty and staff and partnerships with some of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the United States. This university will create a new foundation for Iraq and become the first of its kind in Baghdad, and the largest academic and research institution in the Middle East.
As a family, we believe Iraq deserves to reclaim its place in history as the cradle of civilization. Mesopotamia was the source of all other cultures, the home of the first prophet, and the founder of the world’s first laws.

Much as the sun rises, a new generation must also arise. We believe Iraq can only be rebuilt by its sons and daughters because they carry the hopes and dreams of not only themselves but of those before them, and those after them. It is time to create a new generation of Iraqis who will lead a united nation into the future.

Message from the President

It is a distinct pleasure to welcome you to The American University of Iraq-Baghdad! This institution is modelled after several of the most prestigious American-style universities in the world including the American University of Washington, DC; the American University of Cairo; the American University of Beirut; and the American University of Sharjah. AUIB will be a first-tier institution with the finest faculty and research facilities in the region.

Jeanne Meyers
President

Our Board of Trustees Vision

AUIB will be an example for best practices in education and will be similar to a Tier 1 research university, with a strong focus on teaching, that aims to achieve the highest standards of instruction and research across the entire spectrum of educational programs. The mission is for AUIB to prepare a new generation of leaders for Iraq and the Middle East, who will focus on the needs of the region in each professional field.

 In particular, AUIB will address the role of women in the region, to help each nation develop the untapped potential of women’s talent, initiative, and skill of this fifty-plus percent of the population. AUIB will model its programs after the American system of higher education, utilizing all its best elements in applicable ways across Iraq and the Middle East, and focusing on soft skills, such as team leadership, team membership, communication, collaboration, and problem-solving as key elements of education, in addition to the requisite areas of specific knowledge.
