Why CoP at AUIB?

The College of Pharmacy (CoP) at AUIB has as a sacred mission to develop future leaders in clinical pharmacy practice and related pharmaceutical sciences in Iraq and the broader region, through excellence and innovation in teaching, practice, and research. The College’s state-of-the-art facilities support high-impact research and clinical innovation.

Graduates’ Prospective Careers

The College of Pharmacy (CoP) at AUIB has as a sacred mission to develop future leaders in clinical pharmacy practice and related pharmaceutical sciences in Iraq and the broader region, through excellence and innovation in teaching, practice, and research. The College’s state-of-the-art facilities support high-impact research and clinical innovation.

  • Pharmaceutical industries
  • Research scientists
  • Government and NGO roles
  • Clinical research coordinators/associates
  • Higher education
  • Medical sales and marketing representatives
  • Pharmacologists
  • Clinical toxicologists
  • Regulatory affairs officers
  • Medical science liaisons

CoP Values

  • Equity and Integrity to maintain mutual respect and trust

  • Teamwork and Collaboration to achieve excellence

  • High-impact Leadership in advancing pharmacy education and practice

  • Innovation and Creativity in research and clinical services

  • Continuous and Lifelong Learning through pioneer certificate programs

  • Student Success to recognize and foster talents

Our distinguished programs

Focused on delivery of patient-centered care, our curriculum prepares pharmacy students to be leaders and innovators in this rewarding and dynamic profession, as it keeps abreast of the evolving role of pharmacists. It informs of best practices via award-winning and world-renowned faculty, whose work has impacted students and patients around the world.

CoP currently offers a five-year Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm) program that encompasses extensive basic science education with rigorous and diverse clinical internships, so that graduates are readily prepared for clinical practice in various specialties.

Our Future Programs

CoP is planning to implement soon a six-year Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) program that will provide graduates with advanced clinical skills at our leading-edge university hospital, in various pharmacy specialties.

Several pioneer postgraduate programs in Clinical Pharmacy, Pharmaceuticals Sciences, Pharmacology and Toxicology, and Pharmaceutical Economics and Policy will be launched soon, in partnership with leading universities around the world.

International Professional Membership

CoP is proud to be the only college in Iraq and the region as an institutional member of the following international associations:

  • American Association of Colleges of Pharmacy (AACP)
  • European Association of Faculties of Pharmacy (EAFP)
  • International Pharmaceutical Federation (FIP)

International Partnership

To jointly deliver pioneer continuing education certificate programs exclusively for Pharmacists in Iraq, CoP has signed partnership agreements with leading US associations:

  • American Pharmacists Association (APhA)
  • American Society of Health-System Pharmacists (ASHP)

CoP has also initiated international accreditation for its BPharm program with the American Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE).
