College of Law (CoL)

Home Academies College of Law (CoL)

Why Study at AUIB’s CoL ?

The AUIB College of Law’s mission is to be the leading school offering education in the legal system of Iraq, international law and legal practice, with graduates qualified to practice law in Iraq, join multinational law firms and corporations, and have the analytical skill set to become thoughtful, civically engaged citizens.

Graduates’ Prospective Careers

The Bachelor of Law in Public Law and Private Law program qualifies graduates to work both inside and outside the legal profession. Inside the legal profession, graduates can become Lawyers, Legal Practitioners, Legal Managers, Arbitrators, Legal Writers, Legal Advisors/Experts, Researchers, and_ should they complete higher studies, Academic Lecturers and Professors. Graduates may also be appointed as Judges in Iraq, should they be admitted to the Judicial Institute and graduate from it. Outside the legal profession, graduates of our law program can pursue careers as journalists, management consultants, investment bankers, policy analysts, human resource experts, activists and consultants in the non-profit sector, and members of diplomatic corps.

Our Distinguished Program

CoL is distinguished in its approach, as it provides a curriculum of the future, through continuous assessment, with a foundation of rigorous training in logic, critical thinking, communication, problem solving, teamwork, and research and writing skills. These skills are fundamental to the legal profession, but can also be applied to careers outside of it, making our law degree one of the most versatile degrees for students to pursue.

AUIB’s CoL is distinguished amongst its peers, as its Bachelor of Law program: 

  • Requires bilingual education (Arabic and English) in the legal system of Iraq;
  • Combines American-style liberal education with a rigorous law curriculum that meets all requirements of the Iraqi Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research and the Supreme Judicial Council of Iraq;
  • Boasts Iraqi and American professors, distinguished in their legal fields of expertise;
  • Has state-of-the-art classrooms, labs, a Moot Court, and an international law library with electronic resources;
  • Trains graduates in Alternative Dispute Resolution techniques (Negotiation, Mediation, and Arbitration);
  • Offers opportunities for international study abroad, legal clinics, and internship opportunities.

Faculty

CoL boasts a team of highly competent and culturally diverse international faculty members who hold the highest level of degrees from renowned academic institutions. They are recognized for their outstanding teaching capabilities, well-established scholarship, and international outreach and collaboration. They conduct research at the intersection of law and social science, law and medicine, law and technology, and a variety of other fields. They invite law students to participate in their research, and they mentor law students in the legal clinics, Moot Court competitions, and other College of Law activities.
